Our research

Our research topics cover different aspects of multi-agent systems

  • Distributed Coordination in Multi-Agent Systems

  • Distributed Optimization in Multi-Agent Systems

  • Distributed Estimation in Multi-Agent Systems

  • Robotics and Control Methodologies for Precision Agriculture
