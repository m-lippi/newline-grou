Our research

Our research topics cover different aspects of multi-agent systems

  • Distributed Coordination in Multi-Agent Systems

  • Distributed Optimization in Multi-Agent Systems

  • Distributed Estimation in Multi-Agent Systems

  • Robotics and Control Methodologies for Precision Agriculture

Latest news

  • Our H2020 project CANOPIES (A Collaborative Paradigm for Human Workers and Multi-Robot Teams in Precision Agriculture Systems) has been funded and will be coordinated by Andrea Gasparri
  • Our paper on Distributed estimation and control of node centrality in undirected asymmetric networks appeared in IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control (TAC)
  • Our paper on Multi-Agent Coordination of Thermostatically Controlled Loads by Smart Power Sockets for Electric Demand Side Management appeared in IEEE Transactions on Control Systems Technology (TCST)
  • Our paper on A Novel Observer-Based Architecture for Water Management in Large-Scale (Hazelnut) Orchards has won the Young Author Award at the Agricontrol Conference 2019
  • Our paper on A Distributed Framework for k-hop Control Strategies in Large-Scale Networks Based on Local Interactions appeared in IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control (TAC)
  • Our H2020 Project PANTHEON has made to the news
  • Our paper on Gossip Algorithm for Multi-Agent Systems via Random Walk appeared in Systems & Control Letters (SCL)
  • Our paper on Distributed Flow Network Balancing with Minimal Effort appeared in IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control (TAC)
  • Our paper on Multi-stage Discrete Time and Randomized Dynamic Average Consensus appeared in Automatica
  • Our paper on Decentralized Progressive Shape Formation with Robot Swarms appeared in Autonomous Robots (AURO)
  • The H2020 project PANTHEON focused on the development of a SCADA for the precision farming of hazelnut orchards that Andrea Gasparri is coordinating started on November 1st, 2017
